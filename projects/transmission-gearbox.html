Transmission Gearbox Design

ME 13 Design & Fabrication | 2024

Design Objectives

The goal was to design and manufacture a high-performance transmission achieving 500 RPM while remaining within a $200 budget.

Design Evolution

Feedback during the Critical Design Review (CDR) was vital; we identified a lack of bearings and immediately redesigned the housing to include press-fit support bearings.

  • Configuration: A single gear ratio design utilizing brass gears for durability and spur gear primary transmission.
  • Challenges: Overcame base plate misalignment by redesigning the hole pattern and optimized shaft lengths using manual machining.
  • Performance: Successfully reached 500 RPM with zero mechanical failures during testing.

Specs

Max Speed: 500 RPM
