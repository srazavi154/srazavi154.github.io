Transmission Gearbox Design

ME 13 Design & Fabrication | May 2024

Design Objectives

[cite_start]

The goal was to design and manufacture a high-performance transmission achieving 500 RPM while remaining within a $200 budget[cite: 141, 144].

Design Evolution

[cite_start]

Feedback during the Critical Design Review (CDR) was vital; we identified a lack of bearings and immediately redesigned the housing to include press-fit support bearings[cite: 165].

    [cite_start]
  • Configuration: A single gear ratio design utilizing brass gears for durability and spur gear primary transmission[cite: 152, 154].
    • [cite_start]
  • Challenges: Overcame base plate misalignment by redesigning the hole pattern and optimized shaft lengths using manual machining[cite: 176, 181].
    • [cite_start]
  • Performance: Successfully reached 500 RPM with zero mechanical failures during testing[cite: 193, 195].
