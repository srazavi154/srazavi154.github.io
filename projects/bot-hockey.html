Bot Hockey Competition

ME 72 Design Competition | Finalist

Design Philosophy

I led the design and manufacturing for a fleet of three specialized robots. Rather than identical builds, we mirrored real hockey dynamics with complementary roles.

The Fleet

  • The Enforcer: Features a high-torque drivetrain with 14 N52 neodymium magnets for maximum traction. Scored 9 goals in a single match.
  • The Goalie: Utilizes a chicken wire blocking system and magnetic loading for goal zone anchoring.
  • The Striker: Built with a West Coast Drive (3-wheel system with 1mm center drop) for rapid turning and a flywheel shooter achieving full-court range.

Manufacturing & Material Innovation

We achieved the 48.5 lb team weight limit through strategic pocketing, including a 35% gearbox weight reduction. I transitioned external components from PLA to PETG to gain 20-30 MPa in tensile strength for impact resistance.

Results

The team achieved a podium finish, ultimately losing 1-2 in overtime during the finals due to motor thermal management challenges.
