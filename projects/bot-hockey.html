Manufacturing & Material Innovation

[cite_start]

We achieved the 48.5 lb team weight limit through strategic pocketing, including a 35% gearbox weight reduction[cite: 63]. [cite_start]I transitioned external components from PLA to PETG to gain 20-30 MPa in tensile strength for impact resistance[cite: 75].
