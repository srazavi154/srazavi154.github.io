Melano Metrics

MedTech Venture | Co-Founder & Product Design Lead

The Problem

FDA warnings confirm that pulse oximeters are less accurate for individuals with Fitzpatrick V-VI skin types. This technological gap leads to systematically underestimated blood oxygen levels, contributing to delayed treatment and healthcare disparities in respiratory care and surgical monitoring.

Technical Approach

We developed a solution utilizing ML-powered correction algorithms for commercial wearables and pulse oximeters.

  • Designed and optimized over 20 novel electrode configurations for Electrical Impedance Tomography (EIT)-based diagnostics[cite: 7].
  • Improved ML model accuracy by 30% through iterative hardware-software co-design.
  • Developed a modular data preprocessing pipeline that reduced processing time by 40%.

Business Development

I led the venture's strategic growth by defining a vision that balanced clinical feasibility with commercial viability. This involved crafting a comprehensive investor pitch deck covering technical milestones and regulatory strategies.

Result: Secured $5K in competitive non-dilutive funding via the Caltech Bill Gross Prize for Entrepreneurship.
