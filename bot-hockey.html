Bot Hockey: Team Penguinators

Designing a 3-robot fleet for the 40th Annual ME72 Competition. Speed, magnets, and strategy on a ferromagnetic rink.

Team

The Penguinators

Constraint

48.5 lbs Team Limit

Tech

Arduino, Roboclaw, SolidWorks

Competition Highlights

The Fleet

The Enforcer

The Heavy. 14 Neodymium magnets for max traction. Built to push opponents and clear the rink.

The Goalie

The Wall. Features chicken-wire shielding and a TPU star intake to trap and control the puck.

The Striker

The Sniper. West Coast Drive with a high-speed flywheel shooter and TPU star intake system.

  • Weight Optimization: Pocketed aluminum bellypans using Waterjet & CNC to meet strict limits.
  • Drive Systems: Switched Striker to "West Coast Drive" for 360-degree maneuverability.
  • Smart Control: Custom Arduino code for wheel balancing and "Software Gearing" (High/Med/Low).

Engineering Process

Lessons Learned

Reliability

Simple mechanical fixes (Bevel gears) beat complex electronic ones.

Iteration

Three versions of the "Servo Finger" were needed to stop puck jamming.
