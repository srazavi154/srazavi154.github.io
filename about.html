Welcome to My World!

I’m a Mechanical Engineering graduate from Caltech with a minor in Biology. While I spend a lot of time analyzing mechanics, I’ve always believed that the most elegant solutions come from a broad perspective.

Beyond the lab, I am a Taekwondo Black Belt and an avid explorer of National Parks. I find that the discipline of martial arts and the scale of the natural world both inform the way I approach complex engineering challenges.

My creativity isn't just a hobby—it’s how I process the world. Whether I’m performing Slam Poetry, painting with oils and pastels, or riding horses, I am constantly looking for the "story" in the system. I’m a firm believer in the power of storytelling and the thrill of solving escape rooms; both are just different ways of navigating logic and human experience.

Originally from Louisiana and Virginia, I’m now based in California, always looking for the next problem to solve or the next trail to ride.

"The path isn't a straight line; it's a series of intentional turns."
