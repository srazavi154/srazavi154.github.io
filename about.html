Welcome to My World!

I'm a Caltech Mechanical Engineering graduate with a Biology minor - a builder who believes the best work happens when technical precision meets human meaning. I don't just ask how things work. I ask what they stand for.

Taekwondo Black Belt. National Park wanderer. Travel enthusiast. I've learned discipline through martial arts and perspective through wilderness - both remind me that strength and humility aren't opposites, they're partners.

I'm a Slam Poet, painter, escape room speedrunner, and unapologetic consumer of stories - give me a fantasy novel, a cinematic universe, or a show with twelve seasons and I'm home. I see narratives everywhere: in mechanisms, ecosystems, the arc of a character, and the quiet patterns between machines and hearts. I'm drawn to myth and fiction not as escape, but as blueprints for understanding resilience and purpose.

I build to matter. I create because making things - devices, systems, words, spaces - is how I care for the world. I'm looking for problems that need solving and people who believe good work should leave things a little more just, a little more beautiful, a little more understood.

Louisiana born, Virginia raised, California shaped. Now back in Virginia, ready for what's next.
