Welcome to My World!

I’m a Mechanical Engineering graduate from the California Institute of Technology (Caltech) with a minor in Biology. My work is driven by a passion for creating things that don't just function, but make a difference.

Originally from Baton Rouge, Louisiana, my path took me through Fairfax, Virginia, and eventually to Pasadena, California. Each place added a layer to how I see the world and how I approach design.

Growing up, I've always had a broad range of interests—whether it was tinkering with gadgets, writing poetry, or painting. The common thread? The desire to create.

When I'm not designing or building something in the lab, you can find me spending time with family and friends, exploring new places, or getting lost in a good movie.

"The path isn't a straight line; it's a series of intentional turns."
